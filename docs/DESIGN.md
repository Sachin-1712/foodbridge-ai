# Sharebite Design System (Stitch)

## Core Aesthetics
**Efficient Altruism:** Balances logistical urgency with humanitarian warmth.
**Visual Style:** Corporate Modern with a Tactile edge. Generous white space, structured hierarchy.
**Emotional Response:** Calm capability.

## Colors (Material 3 Extended)
- **Primary (Forest Green):** `#0f5238` / `#2d6a4f` - Growth and sustainability.
- **Secondary (Earth Tan):** `#7d562d` - Grounding and secondary actions.
- **Tertiary (Muted Lime):** `#464a30` - Complementary and supporting elements.
- **Surface:** `#f9f9f6` - Off-white base to reduce eye strain.
- **Background:** `#f9f9f6`
- **Error:** `#ba1a1a`

## Typography
- **Headings:** `Plus Jakarta Sans` - Friendly, modern, optimistic.
- **Body:** `Work Sans` - High legibility, professional.
- **Label Caps:** `Work Sans`, 12px, 600 weight, 0.05em letter spacing.

## Layout & Spacing
- **Grid:** 12-column desktop grid.
- **Baseline:** 4px.
- **Spacing Scale:**
  - `unit`: 4px
  - `xs`: 4px
  - `sm`: 8px
  - `md`: 16px
  - `lg`: 24px
  - `xl`: 48px

## Elevation & Depth (Ambient Shadows)
- **Level 1 (Cards):** `0 4px 12px rgba(45, 106, 79, 0.04)`
- **Level 2 (Active/Hover):** `0 8px 24px rgba(45, 106, 79, 0.08)`
- **Level 3 (Modals/AI):** `0 8px 32px rgba(45, 106, 79, 0.12)`

## Shapes (Roundedness)
- **Standard Components:** 0.5rem (8px)
- **Cards/Large Containers:** 1rem (16px)
- **Pills/Badges:** Full (9999px)

## Component Patterns
- **KPI Cards:** Large centered numbers, small trend indicators, `label-caps` labels.
- **Status Badges:** Fully rounded pills with soft background tints.
- **AI Widgets:** Glassmorphism (`backdrop-filter: blur(12px)`), floating with distinct shadows.
- **Timeline:** Vertical step-indicators with soft connecting lines.
